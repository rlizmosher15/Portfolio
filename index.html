Preliminary Sketches

This is the first stage of any project that I work on. This is where ideas are scribbled, and compositions are tested. 

Value and Lighting Studies

Once a sketch has been chosen to move forward with, I then do a variety of value lighting scenarios to build the proper atmosphere. 

Color Studies

When the light source has been determined and the values have been refined, I create color compositions to refine the mood of the piece. 

Final Rendering

Finally, it's all about refining and rendering. This is where I focus on details, textures, and bring the piece together.

About Me and Jack 

Here you will see me with my partner in crime Jack.

I then transferred to the Academy of Art University online program for visual Development (cause ya know, Covid...). Here I got my AA degree and found a particular love for illustration.

I incorporate eyes as the primary subject in a lot of my work. Mental Illness is a common theme as well.
